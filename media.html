How it all started:

My journey into tech began with a curiosity about how digital products are built and secured at scale. Working as a Technical Support Engineer, I gained firsthand insight into the challenges users and businesses face with technology infrastructures—ranging from system downtime to inefficient deployment practices.

What started as curiosity became a journey of hands-on learning, fast-paced troubleshooting, and constant adaptation. I have grown from managing basic tech issues to confidently supporting complex networks, security protocols, and mission-critical environments. Every ticket taught me patience. Every system failure taught me resilience. And every solution reminded me why I chose this path—to make technology feel reliable, seamless, and human-centered.

How it is going:

As technology evolves, so do I—always learning, always improving, and always ready for what’s next. This experience sparked a deeper interest in understanding the backend of modern systems, especially the cloud environments they run on.

My journey has taught me resilience, creativity, and the power of continuous learning. Whether working solo or with a team, I bring clarity, depth, and passion to every challenge.

Why I Joined ALTSCHOOL Africa:

With a clear goal to deepen my technical expertise, transition fully into cloud, and become a key contributor to building scalable, reliable, and secure systems, I quickly realized that self-learning alone wasn’t enough. I needed structured guidance, a vibrant community, and real-world projects to sharpen my skills and align with global standards. ALTSCHOOL Africa stood out as the right platform to make this transition.

The program’s hands-on, real-world approach—combined with a strong community of learners and mentors—offers the ideal environment for both learning and growth. It aligns with my belief that knowledge should be practical, collaborative, and adaptable to real-world scenarios.
